6. Service Fees and Payment Structure

Our NLV application service for one adult applicant is priced at €1,499 in total, payable in three milestone stages:

  • Milestone 1 — €400: Paid to get started. Covers your eligibility assessment, initial case review, and preparation of your personalised document checklist.
  • Milestone 2 — €400: Paid before document submission. Covers document preparation, review, sworn translations (up to €100), preparation of all consulate forms, and pre-submission dossier checks.
  • Milestone 3 — €699: Paid on visa approval. Covers our support through the approval stage and post-approval guidance.

Our NLV renewal service for one adult applicant is priced at €699 in total, payable in two stages:

  • Stage 1 — €300: Paid to open your renewal case.

Family discount pricing is set out on our Pricing page. All prices are in Euros (€) and are exclusive of any third-party costs (see Section 9 below). Payment is accepted through our secure online dashboard. We accept major debit and credit cards.
