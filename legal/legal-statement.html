2. Nature of Our Services

My Spanish NLV provides administrative preparation, case management, and immigration document support services for persons seeking Spain's Non-Lucrative Visa (NLV) and associated renewals. Our services are operational and administrative in nature and include:

  • Eligibility assessment based on published Spanish immigration law criteria
  • Preparation of a personalised document checklist tailored to your circumstances
  • Document collection, review, and preparation guidance
  • Coordination of official sworn translations of required documents
  • Preparation of all consulate application forms
  • Pre-submission dossier review and consulate appointment briefing
  • Assistance in arranging NLV-compliant private health insurance through our partner services
  • Case management and progress communication via our client dashboard
  • Liaison with Spanish consular authorities during the processing period

Our services do not include Spanish legal advice, tax advice, financial advice, or any professional regulated service under UK law. We do not act as solicitors, regulated immigration advisers, or financial advisers.

3. Immigration Advice — UK Regulatory Position

Important: Retail Consulting UK Ltd is not regulated by the Office of the Immigration Services Commissioner (OISC). Our services relate exclusively to applications for Spain's Non-Lucrative Visa under Spanish immigration law. The OISC regulatory framework applies to advice and services relating to UK immigration matters only. As our services concern applications to Spanish (non-UK) immigration authorities — specifically the relevant Spanish consulate in the applicant's country of residence — they fall outside the scope of OISC regulation.

We are not solicitors and we do not provide legal advice. The information on this website is provided for general informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Nothing on this website creates a solicitor-client relationship between you and Retail Consulting UK Ltd.

If you require formal legal advice in relation to UK immigration law or any other regulated legal matter, you should consult a solicitor or regulated legal professional.

4. Our Legal Partner — Platinum Legal Spain

Where immigration case work or legal representation within Spain is required, this is carried out in collaboration with Platinum Legal Spain, a Spanish legal services firm operating under Spanish law and regulated accordingly in Spain. Platinum Legal Spain is a separately constituted entity and is not a trading name of Retail Consulting UK Ltd.

Retail Consulting UK Ltd provides the administrative preparation and case coordination service; Platinum Legal Spain provides legal expertise and specialist support in relation to Spanish immigration matters as required.

References to "our legal team" or "our specialists" on this website refer to the specialists at Platinum Legal Spain acting in their capacity as our legal partner for Spanish immigration matters.

5. No Guarantee of Outcome

Immigration decisions are made solely by the relevant Spanish consular authority in the applicant's country of residence. My Spanish NLV and Platinum Legal Spain have no influence over the outcome of any application. We cannot guarantee approval of any application, regardless of the quality of the case presented.

Approval depends on factors including the applicant's eligibility at the time of submission, the completeness and accuracy of the documentation provided, and the discretion of the individual consulate. Our milestone fee structure — in which the final stage is payable only on approval — reflects the outcome-linked nature of our service, but does not constitute a guarantee of approval.
