Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026 · My Spanish NLV / Retail Consulting UK Ltd

This cookie policy explains what cookies are, which cookies we use on myspanishnlv.com, and how you can manage your preferences. For broader information on how we handle your personal data, please read our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as language, or whether you have seen a consent banner) over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them when you return or browse between pages.

Cookies can be set by the website you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third-party services that provide functionality on the website ("third-party cookies"). They may be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored on your device for a set period).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly (essential cookies)
  • Understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it (analytics cookies)
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising on platforms such as Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and Google Ads (marketing cookies)
  • Remember your cookie preferences (preference cookies)

3. Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required for the website to work and cannot be switched off. They do not store any personally identifiable information.

Cookie name Type Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Essential Stores your cookie consent preference so you are not asked again on subsequent visits. 12 months
session_id Essential Maintains session state for users who are logged in to the client dashboard. Session
csrf_token Essential Security token to prevent cross-site request forgery attacks on form submissions. Session

3.2 Analytics cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website — which pages are most visited, how long visitors stay, and where they come from. This helps us improve the site. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymised where possible.

Cookie name Type Purpose Duration
_ga Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes unique users by assigning a random number as a client identifier. 2 years
_ga_* Analytics Google Analytics — persists session state across page requests. 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes users; resets daily. 24 hours
_gat Analytics Google Analytics — throttles request rate to limit data collection when traffic is high. 1 minute

We use Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled. Google may process this data in the United States under Google's data processing terms and Standard Contractual Clauses. For more information on how Google processes this data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3.3 Marketing cookies

We use the Meta Pixel (provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.) to measure the effectiveness of our advertising on Facebook and Instagram. These cookies help us understand whether visitors who arrived from one of our adverts went on to take an action on our website, and to build audiences for future campaigns. Meta may process data collected through these cookies in the United States.

Cookie name Type Purpose Duration
_fbp Marketing Meta Pixel — identifies browsers for ad measurement and audience building across Meta's advertising network. 90 days
_fbc Marketing Meta Pixel — stores the click ID when a visitor arrives via a Facebook or Instagram advert, used to attribute conversions back to the relevant ad. 90 days

For more information on how Meta uses this data, see Meta's Privacy Policy. You can manage your Meta ad preferences via Meta Ad Preferences.

3.4 Google Ads cookies

We use Google Ads to run paid search and display advertising. Google Ads cookies allow us to track conversions — whether a visitor who clicked one of our adverts went on to take an action on our website — and to serve relevant ads to people who have previously visited the site. Google may process data collected through these cookies in the United States.

Cookie name Type Purpose Duration
_gcl_au Marketing Google Ads — conversion linker cookie used to store and track conversions from Google Ads campaigns. 90 days
_gcl_aw Marketing Google Ads — stores the Google Click ID (GCLID) when a visitor arrives via a Google Ads link, used to attribute conversions back to the relevant ad. 90 days

For more information on how Google uses this data, see Google's Privacy Policy. You can manage your Google ad settings via Google Ads Settings.

4. Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use:

  • Session recording or heatmap cookies (e.g. Hotjar, Microsoft Clarity)
  • Live chat or customer support widget cookies
  • Cookies set by embedded third-party forms or social media widgets

5. Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit this website, you will be presented with a cookie notice where you can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time.

Managing cookies in your browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some features of this website. For guidance on managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opting out of Google Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Opting out of Google Ads

You can manage your Google ad personalisation settings via Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of Google's use of cookies for ad personalisation by visiting google.com/settings/ads.

Opting out of Meta advertising

You can manage how Meta uses your data for advertising via Meta Ad Preferences. You can also opt out of interest-based advertising from many providers through the Your Online Choices tool (EU/UK).

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our practices. When we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of this website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at hello@myspanishnlv.com or visit our contact page.
