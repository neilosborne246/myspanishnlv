What to Include in Your Spain Non Lucrative Visa Enquiry

The more detail you share upfront, the more useful and specific our response will be. Our immigration specialists can give you much more targeted advice when they understand your individual circumstances. If you're emailing with a question, try to include as many of the following as are relevant.

Helpful information to share with our specialist team

  • ✓ Your nationalityDifferent nationalities may have different consulate requirements or bilateral agreements to be aware of.
  • ✓ Current country of residenceYour consulate is determined by where you currently legally reside — this affects timelines and specific requirements.
  • ✓ Solo or family applicationFamily applications require additional income evidence (~€2,400/month for a single applicant + ~€600/month per family member) and have a family discount.
  • ✓ Approximate monthly income, pension, or savingsThe NLV requires you to demonstrate passive income or savings. Sharing a rough figure helps us assess your eligibility quickly.
  • ✓ Target move dateKnowing your timeline helps us plan your document preparation and consulate appointment scheduling from the start.

Quick Answers

Contact and Spain NLV Enquiry FAQs

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

We reply to all email enquiries within 1 business day, Monday to Friday. Applications started on the dashboard typically receive a specialist response within 4 business hours. Our aim is to give you specific, substantive answers — not holding messages.

Is there a free consultation before I commit?

Yes. When you start on the dashboard, you complete a short eligibility form at no cost and no obligation. A specialist reviews your situation and confirms whether you qualify for the NLV — flagging anything that needs attention before you make any payment. There is no charge for this initial assessment.

Can I call you directly?

We operate as an online service and handle all client communication via the dashboard and email. This allows us to serve clients across multiple time zones effectively, and ensures a clear written record of every piece of advice we provide you. Start on the dashboard or email hello@myspanishnlv.com and we will be in touch promptly.

What information should I include in my Spain NLV enquiry?

For a faster, more specific response, include: your nationality, current country of residence (which determines your consulate), whether you are applying alone or as a family, approximate monthly passive income or savings (the threshold is approximately €2,400/month for a single applicant), and your target move date. The more detail you share, the more useful our initial response will be.

Do I need to visit an office to get started?

No. Everything up to your consulate appointment happens entirely online via the dashboard. You never need to visit our offices — we serve clients from the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and beyond without any in-person meetings. The only in-person step is your consulate appointment in the country where you currently reside. See our how it works page for more detail.
