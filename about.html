Our Story

Why We Built a Specialist NLV Service with Full Transparency at Its Core

My Spanish NLV was founded with a single purpose: to make Spain's Non-Lucrative Visa genuinely accessible to English-speaking applicants worldwide. For too long, the process was the preserve of expensive, opaque traditional firms — clients would hand over thousands of euros with no visibility into what was happening, or when.

We changed that. By building a 24/7 online dashboard at the heart of our service, we give every client full transparency at every stage. You can see your documents, track your application status, communicate with your case manager, and make milestone payments — all from one place, any time of day.

Our team of immigration specialists has guided clients from the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, South Africa, Ireland, and beyond through successful NLV applications. We know the quirks of different consulates, the documents that cause delays, and the exact standards that secure approvals. From understanding NLV requirements to navigating each stage of the application process, we handle it all.

"We do not outsource your case."

Your dedicated case manager handles your application from start to finish — from the initial eligibility check through to visa-in-hand. One team, one point of contact, end-to-end accountability.

We use technology to deliver transparency, but immigration is still a deeply human process. Our specialists are available to answer your questions, brief you before your consulate appointment, and guide you through every decision along the way.

    What is My Spanish NLV and who operates it?

    We are immigration specialists — not lawyers. My Spanish NLV and Platinum Legal Spain operate as specialist immigration services, not a law firm.
