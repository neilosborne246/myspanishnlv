On the Call

What We'll Cover in Your Free Consultation

Your free consultation call is a genuine conversation — not a scripted sales pitch. Bring your questions and we'll work through them together. Here are the areas we cover most frequently.

✅

Eligibility Check

We review your income sources, savings, and personal circumstances to give you an honest assessment of whether you are likely to qualify for the NLV. We will flag any potential issues early — so you can address them before submitting your application.

📋

Document Preparation

We explain exactly which documents you will need for your specific application — including any country-specific requirements that apply at your consulate. We cover apostilles, translations, financial evidence formats, and health insurance requirements.

🗓️

Timing and Process

We give you a realistic, honest timeline — from the day you start preparing documents through to the day you receive your visa and travel to Spain. We factor in your specific consulate's current processing times and any seasonal variations.

🏥

Health Insurance

We explain exactly what kind of health insurance policy you need, what sin copago means in practice, and how to obtain an NLV-compliant policy and certificate. We can refer you to our insurance partner for a quote.

💶

Pricing and Payment

We give you a complete breakdown of our €1,499 full-service fee and how the three staged payments work — €400 to start, €400 at document sign-off, and €699 on visa approval. No hidden costs, no surprises.

💬

Your Questions

Bring anything you want to ask. No question is too basic — we have heard every concern and answered every variation of "but what about my specific situation?". The call is your time to get clarity.
