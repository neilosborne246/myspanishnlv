Key Recommendation

Start your renewal 3-4 months before your NLV expiration date. This gives you time to gather documents, complete payment stages, and submit without rushing.

Preparation Stage

Timeline: 3-4 months before expiration

Important

Do not submit your renewal application more than 1 month early. Consulates may reject early submissions.

2

Documentation & Payments

Timeline: 2 months before expiration

Stage 1 Payment

The €300 Stage 1 fee covers initial processing, document review, and consulate liaison. You'll pay Stage 2 (€399) after approval.

3

Application Submission

Timeline: 1 month before expiration

Processing Time

After submission, expect 4-8 weeks for processing. Consulates may request additional documents or information during this period.

4

Processing & Follow-up

Timeline: 4-8 weeks after submission

Important Dates

Your new NLV will be dated from when you submit the application, not when you receive it. You can legally continue living in Spain after submission, even if processing extends past your old visa expiration date.

5

Final Steps

Timeline: After approval
