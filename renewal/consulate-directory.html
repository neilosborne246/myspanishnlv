Spanish Consulate Directory

Find contact information and hours for Spanish consulates worldwide

Before You Contact

Most consulates require appointments. Many countries now require online appointment bookings through their website or dedicated system. Calling without an appointment may not get results.

Limited hours. Consulates typically operate limited hours, often mornings only. Always check their website for current hours before visiting.

Website is your first stop. For current procedures, appointment systems, and specific requirements, visit your consulate's official website first.
