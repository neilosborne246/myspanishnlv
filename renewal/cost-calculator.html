What's Included in the Cost

Service Fees (€699 total): Our two-stage renewal service—assessment, documentation review, consulate liaison, appointment scheduling, and end-to-end case management through approval.

Government Fees (€50-150): The official consulate fee for processing your renewal application. This is paid directly to the consulate, not to us.

Medical Certificate (€50-300, if required): Only needed if your age or consulate requirements mandate one. Costs vary based on where you get it (Spanish health system, private clinic, or from your home country).

Health Insurance (€100-400/month): Ongoing cost to maintain continuous coverage required for your renewal. Not part of the renewal fee—this is your regular insurance expense.
