Frequently Asked Support Questions

What if I have a question not answered in the renewal guides? ▼

Check our FAQ Master Index which contains 144+ FAQs covering all aspects of renewal. If your question isn't answered there, email us at support@platinumlegalspain.com or schedule a consultation to discuss your specific situation.

How quickly will I get a response to my inquiry? ▼

Email inquiries are answered within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, use our live chat feature for immediate support or call during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm CET). Consultation requests are typically scheduled within 2-3 business days.

Do you offer consultations for clients outside Spain? ▼

Yes! We work with clients around the world. Consultations can be conducted via phone, video call, or email. If you're outside Spain, we typically schedule consultations that work with your timezone. Book a call through our dashboard to arrange a time that suits you.

What languages do you support? ▼

We provide support in English, Spanish, German, French, and Portuguese. When you book a consultation or contact us, simply let us know your preferred language and we'll ensure you work with a team member fluent in that language.

Is there a cost for a consultation? ▼

Your initial consultation is complimentary. We want to understand your situation and discuss whether our service is right for you. If you decide to proceed with our managed renewal service, the consultation fee is waived and counted toward your service fee.

Can I contact you about non-renewal issues? ▼

This support center is specifically for NLV renewal questions. For other visa services or legal inquiries, please visit our main website at myspanishnlv.com or contact our general support team. They can direct you to the appropriate specialist.

What to Include in Your Inquiry

Help Us Help You

To get the fastest and most helpful response, please include:

  • Your current situation (first renewal, second renewal, special circumstances)
  • Your expiry date or timeline
  • The specific issue or question you need help with
  • Any relevant documents or screenshots if applicable
  • Your preferred language and best method to contact you
