What Makes a Successful Renewal

Preparation Leads to Success

Based on our 500+ successful renewals, the key factors that lead to smooth approvals are:

  • Early timing: Start 3-4 months before expiry to avoid pressure
  • Complete documentation: Have all required evidence ready and current
  • Clear financial evidence: Recent bank statements, pension letters, or investment documentation showing you meet income requirements
  • Continuous health insurance: Never let it lapse — this is a common reason for rejection
  • Current housing proof: Lease or property deed showing your Spanish address
  • Professional review: Have a case manager review your dossier before submission to catch issues early
