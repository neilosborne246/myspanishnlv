What is BLS (Serviços de Vistos)?

BLS (Serviços de Vistos) is the official visa application service provider for Spanish embassies and consulates worldwide. BLS is a private company contracted by Spain's Ministry of Foreign Affairs to handle visa applications, document collection, and payment processing.

Key Point: In most countries, you cannot apply directly at the Spanish consulate. You must submit your application through BLS. BLS operates in 150+ countries and handles all Non-Lucrative Visa applications for Spain.

Why Spain Uses BLS

  • Reduces queues at consulates (applications processed off-site at BLS offices)
  • Centralizes document collection and initial verification
  • Enables online appointment booking and status tracking
  • Processes payments securely and separately from consulate
  • Streamlines the entire application workflow

What BLS Does For You

  • Accepts your completed application forms
  • Verifies all documents are present and complete
  • Collects application and processing fees
  • Sends your application to the Spanish consulate
  • Tracks your application status
  • Returns your passport with visa decision (approved or rejected)
  • Provides optional courier services for document delivery

BLS vs Direct Consulate Application

In most countries, BLS is mandatory. You cannot choose to apply directly at the consulate instead. However, a few exceptions exist where direct consulate applications may be possible (this varies by country).

Step-by-Step BLS Application Process

Step 1: Gather Your Documents
Before booking: 2-4 weeks

Collect all required documents for the NLV application (detailed list below). Make copies as instructed. Verify everything is complete before booking your appointment.

Step 2: Book Your BLS Appointment
Same-day to 4 weeks out

Visit your country's BLS booking website (via our directory). Select your preferred location and time slot. Receive a confirmation email with appointment date, time, and location. Print your appointment confirmation.

Step 3: Complete Required Forms
1-2 hours

Fill out the required visa application forms (EX-00 and EX-01 for NLV). Forms must be typewritten (not handwritten in most cases). Sign where required. Print all forms.

Step 4: Attend Your BLS Appointment
30-60 minutes

Arrive 10 minutes early. Bring all original documents and copies as per the checklist. Submit your documents to the BLS official. Pay your visa fee + BLS service fee (cash or card, varies by location). Receive receipt and tracking number.

Step 5: Wait for Consulate Processing
30-60 days (typical)

BLS forwards your application to the Spanish consulate. The consulate reviews your documents. You can track your application status online using your BLS tracking number. Most decisions are made within this window.

Step 6: Receive Your Passport & Visa
5-10 business days

Once the consulate makes a decision, BLS notifies you. Pick up your passport in person (or arrange courier delivery, paid extra) at the BLS office. Your passport will have your NLV visa stamped inside.

Step 7: Travel to Spain & Register
Within 90 days of visa issuance

Travel to Spain before your 90-day window expires. Upon arrival, register at your local town hall (Padron) and book your TIE appointment at the police station within 30 days.

What Documents to Bring to BLS

⚠️ Document requirements vary slightly by country and change periodically. This is a general checklist. Always check your specific country's BLS website for the authoritative requirements.

Original Documents (Bring the actual originals)

  • Passport: Valid for at least 12 months from visa issuance date. Original only.
  • Completed Application Forms: EX-00 and EX-01, signed and dated in original ink
  • Birth Certificate: Original + certified copy (apostille required)
  • Criminal Record Certificate: Issued within the last 3 months, original (apostille required)
  • Proof of Income/Funds: Bank statements, pension statements, investment documentation (originals)
  • Proof of Accommodation: Rental agreement or property deed (original or certified copy)
  • Health Insurance: Original policy document proving NLV-compliant coverage
  • Marriage/Divorce Certificate (if applicable): Original certified copy with apostille

Photocopies (Usually 2-3 sets)

  • Passport (all pages with information)
  • Birth certificate
  • Criminal record certificate
  • Bank statements (first and last pages)
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Health insurance policy (front and back)
  • Marriage/divorce certificate (if applicable)

Documents Requiring Apostille

These official documents must be certified with an apostille (an international certification): birth certificate, criminal record certificate, marriage/divorce certificate, and any university degrees.

Apostille: A special certification stamp that proves a document is authentic according to the Hague Apostille Convention. Apostilles must be obtained from your home country's government office (e.g., Secretary of State in the US, Home Office in the UK). Apostilles typically cost £5-50 and take 1-4 weeks.

Other Items to Bring

  • Your appointment confirmation email (printed)
  • Payment method (cash and/or credit card — check which your BLS accepts)
  • Pen for any forms that require signature
  • Your contact details written down (phone, email, address)

Pro Tip: Use a checklist before your appointment to verify you have everything. Missing even one document can delay your application by weeks. Take photos of all documents before submission as backup.

Application Timeline & Processing Times

Overall Timeline from Start to Visa Issuance

Stage Timeline Details
Preparation 4-8 weeks Gathering documents, getting apostilles, completing forms
BLS Appointment Booking Same-day to 4 weeks Depends on your location and demand at that BLS office
Document Submission at BLS 1 day Your appointment day at BLS office
Consulate Processing 30-60 days Consulate reviews documents and makes decision
Passport Return 5-10 business days After decision, BLS returns your passport with visa
Total (typical) 3-5 months From first BLS booking to receiving visa

Peak vs Off-Peak Seasons

  • Peak Season (Jan-March, Sept-Oct): BLS appointments may take 2-4 weeks to book. Consulate processing can stretch to 60-90 days.
  • Off-Peak (May-August): Shorter wait times for BLS appointments (often same-week). Consulate processing typically 30-45 days.

Tip for Fast Processing: Submit your application as early in the calendar year as possible (January-February). Avoid summer months when consulates are often understaffed and processing slows.

Checking Your Application Status

  1. Save your BLS tracking number (given on submission day)
  2. Visit your country's BLS website
  3. Enter your tracking number and application reference
  4. Check status: "Submitted," "In Process," "Approved," "Rejected"
  5. Status updates typically appear 1-2 weeks after submission

Fees & Costs for BLS Application

What You'll Pay

Fee Type Amount (approx.) Details
Visa Application Fee €60 Spanish government visa fee (non-refundable)
BLS Service Fee €25-60 Varies by country (document handling, processing)
Courier Return Service €15-40 Optional — if you want BLS to courier your passport back (vs picking up in person)
Translation Services €0-100 Optional — if BLS offers document translation services
Total at BLS €85-160 What you pay on appointment day (visa + BLS fee + optional services)

Separate Costs (Not Paid at BLS)

  • Apostille Certification: £5-50 per document (4-8 documents needed)
  • Document Copies: €1-3 for official copies of birth cert, divorce decree, etc.
  • Health Insurance: €500-2,000 per year (separate from visa costs)
  • Travel to Spain: Flights, accommodation, transport costs

BLS Service Fee by Country Examples: UK (£25), USA ($50), Canada ($45), Germany (€35), Australia (A$50), Mexico ($500-600 MXN). Always check your specific country's BLS site for exact fees.

Payment Methods at BLS

  • Cash (in local currency)
  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Bank transfer (in some locations, must arrange in advance)

⚠️ No Refunds: Once you've paid the visa application fee at BLS, it is non-refundable even if your application is rejected. The BLS service fee is also non-refundable in most cases.
